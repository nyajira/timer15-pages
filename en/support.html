Jikan Mitsudo Support

Jikan Mitsudo is a reminder app that sends messages at random intervals between 3 and 30 minutes, helping you feel the day more clearly and fully.

How to Use

  • Open the app and allow notifications.
  • When you start the app, messages will arrive at random intervals between 3 and 30 minutes.
  • Use each message as a small cue to notice what you are doing and how time is passing.

If Notifications Do Not Arrive

  • Open the iPhone Settings app and make sure notifications are allowed for Jikan Mitsudo.
  • Check whether Focus mode or Do Not Disturb is limiting notifications.
  • Quit the app, open it again, and start it once more.

Contact

If you have bug reports or requests, please contact the developer through the App Store review page or the developer's GitHub account.
